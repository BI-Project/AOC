November 11, also known as Pepero Day, is a fun day celebrated in South Korea that is similar to that of Valentine’s Day here in North America. Instead of chocolates, people exchange Pepero, which is a delicious chocolate-coated cookie snack. Giving Pepero to someone is a sign of interest and or affection, which obviously makes it an important day! Curious to see which K-pop idol will be giving you some Pepero this upcoming Pepero Day?
