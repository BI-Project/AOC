On the 19th (KST), K-pop star, Sunmi, uploaded herself wearing DAMWON Gaming’s jersey marked with Heo “ShowMaker” Su’s nickname on her Instagram. According to an official from DAMWON Gaming, they gifted the jersey as they heard that Sunmi is a DAMWON Gaming fan, especially a fan of ShowMaker. Currently, Sunmi is active with her new song, pporappippam.

DAMWON Gaming is having a good run in the 2020 League of Legends Champions Korea Summer Split with an 8-2 (+14) record in second place.
