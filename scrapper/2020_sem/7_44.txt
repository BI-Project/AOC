SEOUL -- K-pop girl band ITZY will come back in August with a new song, the five-member band's agency said.JYP Entertainment said on July 31 that ITZY will release new song "Not Shy" on August 17. The agency said that ITZY members will show off "strong and powerful" charms in the music video for their new song.ITZY has earned the title of "Monster Rookie" when it successfully debuted in February last year. While TWICE, the iconic girl band of JYP, bears a friendly girlfriend-like concept, ITZY shows a girl-crush image by expressing music with strong beats and powerful choreography.
